pub fn with_speed(self, speed: u8) -> Self

Sets the speed of the encoder. Higher speeds come at the cost of lower image quality. Must be between 1 and 30.

Panics
  • Speed is not between 1 and 30.

pub const unsafe fn with_speed_unchecked(self, speed: u8) -> Self

Sets the speed of the encoder. Higher speeds come at the cost of lower image quality. Should be between 1 and 30, but this doesn’t check for that.

Safety

Make sure speed is between 1 and 30.

See Also
  • [with_speed] for the safe, checked version of this method.
